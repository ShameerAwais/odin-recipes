What is Margherita Pizza

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian dish that embodies simplicity and freshness. It features a thin, crispy crust topped with a vibrant tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Optional: Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or as high as it will go.
  2. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Add Tomato Sauce: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Layer with Cheese: Place slices of fresh mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Add Basil: Tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese.
  6. Season: Drizzle olive oil over the top and season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Finish: Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute. Optionally, drizzle with balsamic glaze before slicing.
  9. Serve: Slice and enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!
